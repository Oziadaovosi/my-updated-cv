About

A short version, and a longer one.

I help people work. That's the job in one line — keep the laptop running, the access working, the meeting happening, and the data safe while it does.

I spent six years in financial services handling high-stakes, regulated transactions at Access Bank, where accuracy and confidentiality weren't optional. Since 2024 I've taken that discipline remote, supporting global customers at I'LLTIP across chat, email, and remote-desktop sessions — diagnosing problems, configuring accounts, and writing the kind of internal documentation that means the next ticket is faster to close.

Alongside the support work I've been deliberately retraining. Cisco's Introduction to Cybersecurity, Digital Witch certifications in IT Support and Cloud & DevOps, and a long reading list that's pulling me toward cloud engineering and security operations. The goal is to keep helping people — but from further left in the stack.

Currently focused on

  • Linux administration
  • Cloud foundations (AWS / Azure)
  • Network security basics
  • Scripting for IT automation
  • Documentation systems

How I work
